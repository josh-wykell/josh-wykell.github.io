Improving The World One Line Of Code At A Time

My purpose is to make positive contributions to my community, be it the city I live in, the industry I work in, or the planet we all share.

What I've Figured Out So Far

Learning is rewarding, and problem solving is fun. Finding people the resources they need to do their best work is something I enjoy, and that I'm good at.

This defines how I approach my work. How does this translate to development? Back-End server side development provides endless problems to solve, and as we advance as an industry we create endless learning opportunities. I approach Front-End development as an opportunity to provide people with resources. Software only works if end users know how to utilize programs. I try to approach front end development from this standpoint. From the users' standpoint. Design elements are at their best when they improve the user experience, and are at their worst when they detract from it.
